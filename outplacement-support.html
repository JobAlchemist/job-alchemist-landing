The practical way to support your people through restructure

Help impacted employees move from uncertainty to action with private, structured job-search support they can start using immediately.

What impacted employees get

1

A faster restart

  • Refresh their CV
  • Build a simple first-step plan
  • Prepare an application for the first suitable role
2

Clearer job targeting

  • Understand what roles are really asking for
  • Check fit and identify gaps
  • Tailor CVs and cover letters more effectively
3

Better interview readiness

  • Shape stronger examples from their experience
  • Prepare for likely interview questions
  • Go into interviews more prepared

What HR and employers get

Credible support you can stand behind

A practical support option that is easy to explain and useful from day one.

A more consistent response across impacted employees

Helps avoid a purely ad hoc approach during a high-pressure period.

Less manual support burden on HR and managers

Gives impacted employees a clearer place to start and can reduce repeated requests for help.

A cost-contained alternative where full outplacement is not needed for everyone

Useful when you want to offer real support without the cost and complexity of a fully coached programme for every employee.

Trust and privacy

Employees use JobAlchemist directly and privately. Employers do not see an individual’s job-search activity.

How this differs from traditional outplacement

Traditional outplacement is often built around coaching. JobAlchemist is designed around practical job-search tasks: improving CVs, understanding role fit, tailoring applications, and preparing for interviews. That makes it a useful lighter-weight option where employees need structured support they can use straight away.
