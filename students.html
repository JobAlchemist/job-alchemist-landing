Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to get started?

You need a CV to get CV feedback and build your profile, plus a job ad if you want job analysis, fit insights, and tailored drafts.

How does the CV analysis work?

We look at your CV the way an employer might. We check whether it is clear, easy to read, and relevant for the kinds of jobs you want. We also show where school, sport, volunteering, projects, and other experience can strengthen your application.

How does the job analysis work?

We analyse the job ad and pull together useful public information about the company so you can better understand the role, the workplace, and what the employer may care about most.

How does the fit analysis work?

We compare your CV to the job and show where you match well, what may be missing, and what you could still use as evidence from school, life, or other experience. This helps you decide whether to apply and how to make a stronger case.

How do the CV and cover letter drafts work?

We use your profile and the job ad to create role-specific drafts that are clearer, more relevant, and more believable. You also see what changed and why.

How is this different from just using ChatGPT?

JobAlchemist is built for job applications, not general chat. It keeps your jobs, drafts, and fit insights connected, and gives you step-by-step guidance that is specific to each role.

Will this work if I do not have much job experience?

Yes. That is exactly where it can help. We treat school, sport, volunteering, projects, and other real-life responsibilities as valid experience when they are relevant to the role.

What if I am not a strong fit for a role?

We will tell you clearly and explain why, so you can decide whether to apply, improve your application, or spend your time on a better option.

Will this actually help me stand out?

It can help you apply more clearly and more confidently by showing what matters for the role, what to change, and how to present your experience in a stronger way.

How long does it take?

Usually a few minutes once your CV is uploaded.
