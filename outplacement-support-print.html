What HR and employers get

A cost-contained alternative where full outplacement is not needed for everyone

Trust and privacy

Employees use JobAlchemist directly and privately. Employers do not see an individual’s job-search activity.

How this differs from traditional outplacement

Traditional outplacement is often built around coaching. JobAlchemist is designed around practical job-search tasks: improving CVs, understanding role fit, tailoring applications, and preparing for interviews. That makes it a useful lighter-weight option where employees need structured support they can use straight away.
