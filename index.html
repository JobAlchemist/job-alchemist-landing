Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about JobAlchemist

How does the AI analyse job fit?

We compare your CV and goals to the job description and show where you are strong, what is missing, and why. You get clear fit insights before investing time.

How does the CV and cover letter tailoring work?

You bring the job ad and your base CV. We generate role-specific drafts and explain the changes so you can decide what to keep or refine.

How does the interview prep feature work?

We generate role-specific questions, recommend STAR-based answers from your experience, and run mock interviews with feedback.

How is this different from just using ChatGPT?

JobAlchemist is purpose-built for job seekers. It keeps your roles, drafts, and fit insights connected, so every output is job-specific and confidence-building.

Will this actually help me stand out against other applicants?
