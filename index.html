Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to get started?

A current CV to generate CV Analysis and build your Profile. And a job ad to generate Job Intelligence, fit insights and tailored drafts.

How do you analyse the CV or Resume?

We parse your CV into structured skills and achievements, then run a recruiter-style scan for clarity, ATS readability, gaps, and narrative strength, benchmarking against current market expectations. You get specific fixes and example rewrites tailored to you.

How does Job Intelligence analysis work?

We analyse the job ad, then pull in current public signals about the company to surface the real mission, risks, and success profile behind the listing. You get a clear narrative, hidden expectations, and practical interview angles tailored to that role.

How does the AI analyse job fit?

We compare your CV to the job’s real requirements, looking beyond keywords to map transferable skills, seniority alignment, and likely hiring‑manager concerns. The output highlights your strongest angles, critical gaps backed by evidence, and the narrative that will win the interview.

How does the CV and cover letter tailoring work?

We use your structured profile plus the job ad’s language to reshape your CV and cover letter around the role’s real priorities, not generic templates. You get a role‑specific narrative, targeted keywords, and a clear change log explaining what was adjusted and why.

How does the interview prep feature work?

This is coming soon, tell us if you want it early. We’ll generate role‑specific questions, suggest STAR‑based answers from your experience, and may offer mock interview practice with feedback.

How is this different from just using ChatGPT?

JobAlchemist is purpose‑built for job seekers, keeping your roles, drafts, and fit insights connected so every output is job‑specific, deeper, and more confidence‑building. Soon you’ll be able to review all opportunities in one place to make smarter decisions across your search.

Is my email and data safe?

We use Supabase for secure data storage and access control, including row‑level security so each user only sees their own data. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by our cloud providers, and we follow least‑privilege access.

How long does it take to generate an application pack?

Typically minutes once your CV is in, because everything is built around a specific role.

Do you support different regions and CV formats and roles?

Yes. We adjust for regional expectations (resume vs CV) and local norms. JobAlchemist automatically tailors to specific roles and seniority.

What if I’m not a strong fit for a role?

We’ll tell you directly and explain why, so you can decide whether to invest time.

Will it work for career switchers?

Yes. We map transferable skills to the role and help you position the story clearly.

Will this actually help me stand out against other applicants?

Yes. It saves time by turning each job into a clear, guided workflow, and boosts confidence by explaining what to change and why so you know you’re applying with a stronger, role‑specific narrative.
